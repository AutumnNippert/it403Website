Hollow Body Guitar

Purpose

The hollow cavity in the body of the guitar creates a
warm resonant sound, but is not loud enough to not need
an amplifier. This resonance can add unwanted feedback.
Compare to the Solid Body guitar.

Where it's used

Some places Hollow Body guitars are used are

  • Jazz
  • Blues
  • Rock
    • Beatles
