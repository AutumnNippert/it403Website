Solid Body Guitar

Purpose

The lack of any resonant cavity in the guitar leads to a
sharper toned guitar that needs an amplifier to sound.
Compared to the Hollow Body guitar, the solid body
has more sustain for the notes and less unwanted feedback.

Where it's used

Some places Solid Body guitars are used are

  • Country
  • Rock
  • Metal

Most popular kinds of Solid Body Guitars

  1. Stratocaster
  2. Telecaster
  3. Les Paul
  4. SG
