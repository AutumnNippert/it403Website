Semi-Hollow Body Guitar

Purpose

The partial hollow cavity in the body of the guitar creates a
warm resonant sound, but keeps most of the perks of the
Solid Body guitar.
It does require an amplifier.
Compare to the Solid Body guitar and the Hollow Body guitar.

Where it's used

Some places Semi-Hollow Body guitars are used are

  • Jazz
  • Blues
  • Soft Rock
  • Country
