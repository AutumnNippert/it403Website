Acoustic Guitar

There are 2 main kinds of Acoustic guitars

Acoustic Steel String

Purpose

The hollow cavity in the body of the guitar creates a
warm sound while the strings add a lot of treble.
This guitar does not need an amplifier, but some are able
to utilize them if needed.

Where it's used

Some places Acoustic Steel String guitars are used are

  • Country
  • Folk
  • Jazz
  • Bluegrass

classical Nylon String

Purpose

The hollow cavity in the body of the guitar creates a
warm sound and the strings accompany that fact.
The thinner nylon strings sound much warmer than their
electric counterpart, leading to a warmer tone.
This guitar does not need an amplifier, but some are able
to utilize them if needed.

Where it's used

Some places classical Nylon string guitars are used are

  • classical
  • Flamenco
